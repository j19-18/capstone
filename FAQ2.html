Frequently Asked Questions

What is Filmaniac? +

Filmaniac is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, documentaries, and more on thousands of internet-connected devices.

You can watch as much as you want, without a single commercial.

Where can I watch? +

Watch anywhere on cinemas, anytime. Sign in with your Filmaniac account for reservation.

What can I watch on Filmaniac? +

Filmaniac has an extensive library of feature films, documentaries, TV shows, anime, award-winning originals, and more. Watch as much as you want, anytime you want.

How do I view what's in my Purchased? +

To view what's in your Purchased, click on Purchase icon in the upper-right corner the screen. This will show all the items you've purchased. You can easily change the number of quantities you purchase for a particular item by updating the quantity listed.

How do I remove selected items from my purchase? +

To remove items from your Purchase, go to Home page page by clicking on the Purchase icon on the upper corner of the screen. Once youre there, identify the item you would like to remove and then click on Remove button.
